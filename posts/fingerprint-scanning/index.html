    Fingerprint Scanning Q&A

    September 11, 2013 by Jason Lollback

    Great Q&A about Fingerprint Scanning by Rich Mogull on the technology and security improvements provided by fingerprint scanners.

    "Practically speaking, for most consumers, a fingerprint is more secure than a passcode on your iPhone. It’s definitely more secure than a four-digit passcode."

    Its going to take some time before we're all using devices with fingerprint scanners, so until then I'll continue to keep my devices secured with a 4 digit passcode.
